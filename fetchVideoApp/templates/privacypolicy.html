Privacy Policy


Your Privacy Matters: We collect minimal data and do not store your downloaded content or personal videos.

Last updated: September 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how FetchVideo ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our YouTube video downloading service.

What We Collect

Minimal Data Collection

We believe in minimal data collection. When you use FetchVideo, we may collect:

  • IP Address: Automatically collected for service functionality and security
  • Browser Information: Browser type and version for compatibility
  • Usage Statistics: Basic analytics about service usage (anonymous)
  • YouTube URLs: Only temporarily processed to fetch video information
What We DON'T Collect: Personal information, email addresses, names, payment details, or any user content.

How We Use Your Data

We use the minimal data we collect only for:

  • Providing the video downloading service
  • Maintaining service security and preventing abuse
  • Improving service performance and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations

Data Storage & Security

No Content Storage

Important: FetchVideo does not store, host, or save any video content. We are simply a tool that facilitates downloads from YouTube's servers to your device.

  • Video files are downloaded directly from YouTube to your device
  • No videos are stored on our servers
  • No user-generated content is retained
  • YouTube URLs are only processed temporarily

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the limited data we collect:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited data retention periods
  • No sale of personal data to third parties

Cookies & Tracking

We may use minimal cookies for:

  • Service functionality
  • Security purposes
  • Anonymous usage analytics

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-Party Services

FetchVideo integrates with YouTube's API and servers. When you download videos:

  • Data is transmitted directly between YouTube and your device
  • We do not intermediate the actual video content transfer
  • YouTube's privacy policy applies to their content and services

User Responsibilities

Remember: You are responsible for ensuring you have legal rights to download and use any content.

By using FetchVideo, you agree to:

  • Use the service only for lawful purposes
  • Respect copyright laws and content ownership rights
  • Not use the service for illegal activities
  • Only download content you own or have permission to download

Data Retention

We retain minimal data only as long as necessary for:

  • Service operation (typically 30 days or less)
  • Legal compliance requirements
  • Security and abuse prevention

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we may hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Contact us about privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • Updates posted on this page
  • Clear notices about changes
  • Updated "Last updated" date
